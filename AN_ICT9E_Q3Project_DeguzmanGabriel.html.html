Fire Prevention Tips

Tip Description
Install Smoke Alarms Smoke alarms can detect a fire early, providing enough time to escape. Install smoke alarms in every room of your home and test them regularly to ensure they are working properly.
Keep Flammable Materials Away Keep flammable materials like curtains, papers, and furniture away from heat sources like stoves and heaters to prevent fires from starting.
Practice Safe Cooking Habits Never leave cooking food unattended, and keep flammable objects away from the stove. Turn off the stove and oven before leaving the kitchen.
Have an Escape Plan Make sure everyone in your home knows how to escape in case of a fire. Plan and practice escape routes with your family.
